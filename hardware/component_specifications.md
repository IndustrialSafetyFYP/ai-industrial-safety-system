# Hardware Component Specifications & Pricing

## Microcontrollers

### ESP32 DevKit V1
**Purpose:** Sensor node microcontroller
**Specifications:**
- Dual-core 32-bit LX6 microprocessor
- Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n
- Bluetooth 4.2 + BLE
- 34 GPIO pins
- 4MB Flash memory

**Pakistani Market Pricing:**
- Daraz.pk: PKR 1,200-1,500 per unit
- Hafeez Center: PKR 1,000-1,300 per unit
- Required: 4 units (3 sensor nodes + 1 spare)

### Raspberry Pi 4 Model B (4GB)
**Purpose:** Central hub for AI processing
**Specifications:**
- Broadcom BCM2711 quad-core Cortex-A72
- 4GB LPDDR4 RAM
- 2.4 GHz and 5.0 GHz Wi-Fi
- Bluetooth 5.0
- Gigabit Ethernet

**Pakistani Market Pricing:**
- Online stores: PKR 18,000-20,000
- Local markets: PKR 16,000-18,000

## Gas Sensors

### MQ-2 Sensor (LPG, Propane, Smoke)
**Purpose:** Detect LPG and combustible gases
**Specifications:**
- Detection: 200-10,000 ppm
- Operating voltage: 5V DC
- Analog output: 0-5V
- Heater consumption: ~800mW

**Pakistani Market Pricing:**
- PKR 300-400 per sensor
- Required: 4 units

### MQ-7 Sensor (Carbon Monoxide)
**Purpose:** Detect CO gas
**Specifications:**
- Detection: 20-2000 ppm CO
- Operating voltage: 5V DC
- Heater cycle: 60s @ 5V, 90s @ 1.4V

**Pakistani Market Pricing:**
- PKR 400-500 per sensor
- Required: 4 units

### MQ-4 Sensor (Methane/Natural Gas)
**Purpose:** Detect methane/CNG
**Specifications:**
- Detection: 300-10,000 ppm methane
- High sensitivity to natural gas
- Fast response time: <10 seconds

**Pakistani Market Pricing:**
- PKR 450-550 per sensor
- Required: 4 units
