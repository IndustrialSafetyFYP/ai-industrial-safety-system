# AI-Powered Industrial Safety System - Problem Statement

## The Problem in Pakistan
Industrial accidents due to gas leaks are common in Pakistan, but affordable intelligent safety systems don't exist.

### Current Issues:
- Gas detectors only alarm AFTER dangerous levels are reached
- Imported smart systems cost $2000+ - too expensive for local businesses
- No predictive capabilities to stop accidents before they happen
- Workers don't get mobile alerts in real-time

## Our Solution
We will build an AI-powered safety system that:

### What it does:
- Uses ESP32 microcontrollers with gas sensors
- Implements machine learning to detect leaks EARLY
- Sends real-time alerts to mobile phones
- Can automatically shut off gas supply
- Costs under $200 per unit (90% cheaper)

### Who will use it:
- CNG stations
- Restaurant kitchens
- Textile factories
- Chemical plants

### Technical Components:
- **Hardware:** ESP32, MQ-2/MQ-7 gas sensors, Raspberry Pi
- **Software:** Python, AI model, Flutter mobile app
- **Features:** Real-time monitoring, predictive alerts, automatic shutdown

## Why This Project Matters
This can save lives, prevent accidents, and create a business opportunity for us after graduation.
